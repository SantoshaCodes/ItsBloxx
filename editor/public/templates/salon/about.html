Our Story

Crafting Beauty in Portland Since 2017

From a single styling chair to Portland's most loved beauty studio, our journey is driven by passion, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to making every client shine.

Our Journey Founded June 2017

From Passion to Purpose

Luxe Beauty Studio was born from a dream to create a space where beauty and artistry converge. Founded in 2017 by Sofia Reyes, a master colorist with over a decade of experience at top salons in New York and Los Angeles, the studio began as a boutique two-chair salon in Portland's Pearl District. Sofia saw a gap in Portland's beauty scene: clients deserved a studio that combined technical excellence with a genuinely luxurious, relaxing experience. Today, Luxe Beauty Studio has grown to a team of 12 talented stylists and estheticians, each handpicked for their artistry and dedication to continuing education. We invest in premium products, stay ahead of trends, and treat every appointment as an opportunity to make someone feel extraordinary. From first haircuts to wedding-day glam, we've been trusted with over 2,000 clients' most important beauty moments. Our philosophy is simple: listen deeply, execute flawlessly, and ensure every guest leaves feeling like the best version of themselves.
