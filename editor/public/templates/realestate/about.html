Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Results

Founded in 2009, Keystone Realty was born from a simple belief: buying or selling a home should be an exciting milestone, not a stressful ordeal. Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, started the agency after years of watching families struggle with impersonal, transaction-focused brokerages that treated clients like numbers on a spreadsheet.

Today, Keystone Realty is one of Portland's top-performing independent agencies, with a team of dedicated agents who live and breathe the local market. We've helped over 1,200 families find their dream homes, and we've closed more than $250 million in residential and investment property transactions.

What sets us apart is our commitment to education and transparency. We believe informed clients make confident decisions, which is why we invest time explaining every step of the process, from initial consultation through closing day and beyond.
