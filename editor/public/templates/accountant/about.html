Our Story

Trusted Financial Guidance Since 2004

From a two-person practice to Denver's most trusted accounting firm, our journey is driven by one mission: helping clients achieve lasting financial confidence.

Our Journey Founded June 2004

Built on Integrity, Driven by Results

Summit Financial Group was founded with a clear purpose: to provide small businesses and individuals with the same caliber of financial expertise typically reserved for large corporations. What began in 2004 as a two-person CPA practice in downtown Denver has grown into a full-service accounting firm serving over 500 clients across Colorado. Our founding partners, Katherine Chen and David Morales, both left Big Four firms because they believed accounting should be personal, proactive, and accessible. That philosophy still guides everything we do today. We don't just file your taxes -- we become true financial partners, understanding your goals, anticipating challenges, and building strategies that create real value. Every client receives a dedicated CPA who knows their situation inside and out. With over 20 years of combined experience, advanced certifications, and a commitment to staying current with ever-changing tax laws, Summit Financial Group delivers the precision, insight, and peace of mind your finances deserve.
