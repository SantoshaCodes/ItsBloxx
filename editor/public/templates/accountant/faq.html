FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our accounting services, pricing, and process. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

We offer a comprehensive range of financial services including tax preparation for individuals and businesses, bookkeeping, payroll processing, business advisory, IRS audit support, and financial planning. Each service is tailored to your specific needs and delivered by licensed CPAs with decades of experience.

Our pricing depends on the complexity of your needs and the services you require. We offer transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Individual tax returns typically start at $300, while business services are customized based on scope. We provide a free initial consultation where we assess your needs and provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

Tax software works well for simple W-2 returns with standard deductions. However, if you're self-employed, own a business, have investment income, rental properties, or complex deductions, a CPA can identify significant savings that software typically misses. Our clients save an average of $3,200 more per year compared to self-filing.

For your free initial consultation, bring your most recent tax returns (personal and business), current financial statements, a list of your financial goals or concerns, and any specific questions you have. If you're a business owner, bring your profit and loss statement and balance sheet. Don't worry if you don't have everything -- we'll guide you through what's needed.

We recommend quarterly meetings at minimum to review your financial position, plan for upcoming tax deadlines, and make strategic adjustments. Many of our business clients meet monthly to review bookkeeping and discuss cash flow. Your dedicated CPA is also available year-round via phone and email for any questions that arise between meetings.

Absolutely. We provide full IRS audit representation, which means we handle all communication with the IRS on your behalf. Our team prepares the necessary documentation, responds to requests, and represents you in person if needed. We have a strong track record of resolving audits favorably and minimizing penalties.

Yes, we work with clients across the United States. While our office is in Denver, we serve businesses and individuals in all 50 states through secure digital collaboration tools. We're experienced in multi-state tax filings and can handle the complexity of operating across state lines.

Data security is a top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data transmission and storage, multi-factor authentication for client portal access, and maintain SOC 2 compliant systems. All team members undergo annual security training. We never share client information with third parties without explicit consent and comply with all IRS data protection requirements.
