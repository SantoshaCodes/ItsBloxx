Our Story

Passion for Visual Storytelling

Frame & Focus was founded in 2015 with a simple belief: every person, every family, and every moment deserves to be captured with artistry and intention. What began as a one-person operation has grown into a team of three talented photographers, each bringing a unique perspective and specialty.

We believe photography is more than clicking a shutter. It's about creating a safe, relaxed environment where genuine emotion can surface. Our approach combines technical expertise with an editorial eye, resulting in images that feel both timeless and authentic.

Based in Portland, Oregon, we serve clients throughout the Pacific Northwest and are available for destination work worldwide. Our studio is a welcoming space equipped with professional lighting, backdrops, and a client lounge where you can feel at home.
