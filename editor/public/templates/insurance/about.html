Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Service

Shield Insurance Group was founded in 2001 by Robert Chen with a simple mission: make insurance understandable and accessible for every family and business in Texas. Frustrated by the complexity and impersonal nature of large insurance companies, Robert set out to build an agency where clients are treated like neighbors, not numbers.

Today, our team of licensed agents represents over 20 top-rated carriers, allowing us to compare plans and find the best coverage at competitive prices. We specialize in home, auto, life, business, health, and umbrella insurance, serving more than 10,000 policyholders across Texas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to being there when it matters most. From helping you choose the right policy to guiding you through the claims process, our agents provide hands-on support every step of the way.
