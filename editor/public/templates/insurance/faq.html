FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about insurance coverage, claims, and policies.

We offer comprehensive coverage including home insurance, auto insurance, life insurance, business insurance, health insurance, and umbrella insurance. As an independent agency, we represent over 20 top-rated carriers, allowing us to compare plans and find the best coverage at competitive prices for your specific needs.

Most clients save between 15% and 25% by bundling their home and auto policies with the same carrier. On average, our clients save approximately $1,200 per year through bundling and other available discounts. The exact savings depend on your coverage levels, location, driving record, and the carrier selected.

You can file a claim by calling our office at (512) 555-0147 during business hours, or by contacting your carrier directly using the number on your insurance card. We recommend calling us first so we can guide you through the process, help you document the damage, and advocate on your behalf. Our team follows up on every claim to ensure it is handled promptly and fairly.

Several factors influence your home insurance rate, including your home's age, construction type, location, replacement cost, and proximity to fire stations. Your claims history, credit score, deductible amount, and the coverage limits you choose also play a role. Installing security systems, smoke detectors, and upgrading your roof can help lower your premium.

Umbrella insurance is recommended for anyone with significant assets to protect, a swimming pool, a teenage driver, pets, or rental properties. It provides an extra layer of liability coverage beyond your home and auto policies, typically starting at $1 million for just $150 to $300 per year. If you are ever sued for damages exceeding your standard policy limits, umbrella insurance covers the difference.

Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period (typically 10, 20, or 30 years) and pays a death benefit only if you pass away during that term. It is more affordable and straightforward. Whole life insurance provides lifelong coverage and includes a cash value component that grows over time, but comes with higher premiums. Our agents can help you determine which type best fits your financial goals and family situation.

Most quotes can be prepared within 24 hours of receiving your information. For straightforward home and auto policies, we can often provide preliminary quotes during your initial phone call or meeting. Business insurance and specialized coverage may take a few additional days depending on the complexity of your operations and coverage needs.

Yes, you can make changes to your insurance policy at any time. Common mid-term changes include adding a new vehicle, increasing coverage limits, adding a rider for expensive jewelry or equipment, or updating your address after a move. Some changes may result in a premium adjustment. Contact our office and we will process the change and explain any cost differences.
