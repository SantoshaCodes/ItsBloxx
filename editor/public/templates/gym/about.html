Our Journey Founded March 15, 2018

From Passion to Purpose

IronForge Fitness was born from a simple yet powerful vision: create a gym where everyone feels welcome, supported, and empowered to achieve their fitness goals. Founded in 2018 by Alex Rivera and Jordan Kim, two former competitive athletes who saw a gap in Portland's fitness landscape, our gym began as a modest 3,000 square foot facility with just 50 founding members. Today, we've grown to over 500 active members, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe fitness should be accessible 24/7, training should be backed by science and expertise, and every member deserves personalized attention regardless of their fitness level. Our community-first approach means celebrating every milestone together, from first pull-ups to marathon PRs.
