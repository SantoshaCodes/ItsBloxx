Our Story

From a Small Crew to Denver's Most Trusted Contractor

Summit Builders was founded in 2004 by Mark Callahan, a third-generation builder who grew up watching his grandfather frame houses in the Colorado foothills. What started as a three-person crew working on small residential projects has grown into a full-service general contracting firm with a team of 35 skilled professionals.

Over the past two decades, we have completed more than 200 projects ranging from custom home builds and kitchen renovations to large-scale commercial buildouts. Our commitment to transparent pricing, on-time delivery, and exceptional craftsmanship has earned us a 4.8-star rating and the trust of homeowners and businesses across the Denver metro area.

We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Every project is backed by our workmanship warranty and managed by a dedicated project manager who keeps you informed from demolition to final walkthrough.
