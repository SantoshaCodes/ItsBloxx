Our Story

Built on Trust, One Pipe at a Time

FlowRight Plumbing was founded in 2004 by master plumber Robert Chen with a simple mission: provide honest, high-quality plumbing service at fair prices. What started as a one-truck operation has grown into Portland's most trusted plumbing company with a team of 15+ licensed professionals.

We believe every home deserves reliable plumbing, and every customer deserves respect. That means showing up on time, explaining the problem clearly, providing upfront pricing, and standing behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee.

Today, FlowRight serves residential and commercial clients across the greater Portland metro area, from emergency repairs to planned renovations. Our commitment to ongoing training ensures our team stays current with the latest plumbing technologies and best practices.
