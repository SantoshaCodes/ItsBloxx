Core Features

Built for the Way You Work

Six powerful modules that work together seamlessly.

Project Management

Kanban boards, Gantt timelines, and sprint planning in one view. Assign tasks, set deadlines, track dependencies, and monitor progress across unlimited projects. Custom fields let you adapt CloudSync to any workflow your team uses.

Team Collaboration

Real-time document editing, threaded comments, and instant messaging built right in. Share files, tag teammates, and keep every conversation linked to the work it's about. No more context-switching between apps.

Workflow Automation

Build automations with a visual drag-and-drop editor. Auto-assign tasks, send notifications, update statuses, and trigger actions across your tools. Save hours every week on repetitive processes.

Analytics Dashboard

Track velocity, burndown, cycle time, and custom KPIs in real time. Shareable dashboards give leadership visibility without interrupting the team. Export reports as PDF or CSV with one click.

Integrations

Connect 200+ tools including Slack, GitHub, Jira, Google Workspace, Salesforce, and Zapier. Two-way sync keeps everything up to date automatically. Setup takes minutes, not days.

API Access

Full RESTful API with webhooks, SDKs for Python, Node.js, and Go, plus a sandbox environment for testing. Build custom integrations and extend CloudSync to fit your exact needs.
