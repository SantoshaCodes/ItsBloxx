Our Story

Born from a Real Problem

CloudSync started in 2021 when three engineers at a fast-growing startup realized their team was drowning in disconnected tools. Tasks lived in spreadsheets, communication happened across five different apps, and nobody had a clear picture of project status.

They built CloudSync to solve their own problem: one platform where teams can plan work, collaborate in real time, and automate the tedious stuff. What started as an internal tool quickly gained traction when other companies asked to use it.

Today, CloudSync powers 2,400+ teams across 45 countries. Our mission remains the same: eliminate the friction between having an idea and shipping it. We're backed by leading investors and growing fast, but we're still a team of builders who care deeply about making work feel less like work.
