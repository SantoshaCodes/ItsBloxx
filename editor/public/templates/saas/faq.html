Everything you need to know about CloudSync

CloudSync is an all-in-one SaaS platform for project management, team collaboration, and workflow automation. It helps teams plan work, communicate in real time, and automate repetitive tasks — all from a single workspace. Whether you're a 5-person startup or a 500-person enterprise, CloudSync adapts to how your team works.

Sign up with your email — no credit card required. You get full access to all Professional plan features for 14 days. After the trial, choose a paid plan or your workspace becomes read-only. No data is ever deleted, and you can reactivate at any time.

Yes. CloudSync supports one-click imports from Trello, Asana, Jira, Monday.com, and Basecamp. You can also import via CSV or use our API for custom migrations. Our support team is happy to assist with large-scale migrations for Enterprise customers.

CloudSync is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We perform regular third-party penetration testing and offer SSO/SAML authentication on Enterprise plans. Our security page has full details.

CloudSync connects with 200+ tools including Slack, GitHub, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, Figma, and Zapier. All integrations feature two-way sync and take minutes to set up. We add new integrations every month based on customer feedback.

Yes. Our RESTful API is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. We provide comprehensive documentation, SDKs for Python, Node.js, and Go, webhooks for real-time events, and a sandbox environment for safe testing. Rate limits are generous and scale with your plan.

All plans include email support and access to our comprehensive help center with guides, tutorials, and video walkthroughs. Professional plans add live chat with average response times under 5 minutes. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager, priority support, and custom onboarding sessions.
