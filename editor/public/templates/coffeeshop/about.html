About Us

A Cup of Coffee Can Change Your Whole Day

The Daily Grind started in 2018 as a weekend pop-up cart at the Portland Saturday Market. Founder Elena Vasquez had spent years traveling Central and South America, building relationships with small-lot coffee farmers and learning the art of roasting. She dreamed of bringing those flavors and stories to her Portland neighborhood.

Within a year, the cart had a loyal following. In 2019, we opened our brick-and-mortar location on NW Everett Street, transforming a century-old warehouse into the warm, inviting space you see today. Exposed brick, reclaimed wood tables, and the constant hum of our La Marzocca espresso machine make it feel like home.

Today, we source beans directly from farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, Guatemala, and beyond. Every bag is roasted in small batches at our Pearl District roastery. We believe great coffee starts with great relationships, and every cup we serve tells the story of the people who grew it.

Our Team

Meet the People Behind the Bar

Passionate baristas and coffee nerds who make every cup count.

Elena Vasquez

Founder & Head Roaster

Former agronomist turned roaster, Elena spent three years living on coffee farms in Colombia and Guatemala. She oversees all sourcing and roasting, ensuring every bean meets her exacting standards. SCA-certified Q Grader.

Marcus Chen

Lead Barista

Three-time Portland Latte Art champion, Marcus brings precision and creativity to every drink. He leads our barista training program and hosts monthly latte art workshops for the community.

Sophie Andersson

Pastry Chef & Operations

Classically trained at Le Cordon Bleu, Sophie creates our rotating menu of pastries and savory bites using seasonal, locally sourced ingredients. She also manages catering and private events.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make, from farm to cup.

Ethically Sourced

We pay above Fair Trade prices and maintain direct relationships with every farm we buy from. Transparency from seed to cup.

Sustainability First

Compostable cups, zero single-use plastics, and a 10-cent discount when you bring your own mug. Small steps, big impact.

Community Driven

We host open mic nights, art shows, and coffee education workshops. The Daily Grind is your living room away from home.

Come Meet the Team

We love putting faces to names. Stop by and say hello — your first drip coffee is on us.
