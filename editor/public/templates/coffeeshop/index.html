What We Serve

Crafted with Care, Every Cup

From espresso to catering, we bring passion and quality to everything we do.

Espresso Drinks

Classic lattes, cappuccinos, cortados, and seasonal specials made with our house-roasted espresso blend, pulled to perfection by expert baristas.

Pour-Over Coffee

Single-origin beans brewed one cup at a time using Hario V60 and Chemex methods, highlighting the unique flavor profiles of each origin.

Cold Brew

Slow-steeped for 24 hours to create a smooth, naturally sweet cold brew available on tap. Try it with oat milk or our house-made vanilla syrup.

Pastries & Food

Fresh-baked croissants, muffins, scones, and savory breakfast sandwiches sourced from local Portland bakeries and made with seasonal ingredients.

Catering

Office meetings, workshops, and events deserve great coffee. We offer full-service catering with urns, pour-over bars, and pastry platters.

Private Events

Book our cozy back room for latte art classes, coffee cuppings, birthday parties, or creative meetups. Capacity for up to 30 guests.
