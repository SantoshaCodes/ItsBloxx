Our Story

The Heart Behind Ember & Oak

Handcrafted with intention. Designed for everyday beauty.

Founded in 2019

From a Small Workshop to Your Home

Ember & Oak started in a converted garage in Portland, Oregon, with a simple belief: the objects we surround ourselves with should be beautiful, functional, and made with care. What began as a weekend candle-making hobby between friends quickly became a mission to bring artisan-quality home goods to people who value craftsmanship over convenience.

Today, we work with a network of twelve local artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every candle is hand-poured, every ceramic piece is thrown on a wheel, and every textile is woven on a loom. We source our materials from ethical suppliers and use recycled, compostable packaging for every order.

Our Team

Meet the Founders

Three friends united by a love of craft and community.

Maya Chen

Co-Founder & Head of Design

A ceramicist with 15 years of experience, Maya shapes the aesthetic vision of every Ember & Oak collection. She trained at the Oregon College of Art & Craft.

Leo Alvarez

Co-Founder & Master Chandler

Leo hand-pours every candle using 100% soy wax and essential oil blends he develops himself. His signature scents have won two regional craft awards.

Priya Nair

Co-Founder & Operations Lead

Priya manages artisan partnerships and supply chain. Her background in sustainable business ensures every decision aligns with the brand's eco-conscious values.
