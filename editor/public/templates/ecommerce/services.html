What We Offer

Services Designed Around You

From shipping to custom orders, we go the extra mile.

Free Shipping

Enjoy complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $75. We ship within 1-2 business days, and most domestic orders arrive within 5-7 days. Expedited and international shipping options are also available at checkout.

  • Free over $75
  • 1-2 day processing
  • Tracking included

Gift Wrapping

Make any order extra special with our complimentary gift wrapping service. Each gift is wrapped in our signature recycled kraft paper, tied with a linen ribbon, and finished with a sprig of dried lavender. Add a handwritten note at checkout.

  • Complimentary wrapping
  • Handwritten note option
  • Eco-friendly materials

Loyalty Program

Join the Ember Circle and earn 1 point for every dollar you spend. Redeem points for discounts, get early access to new collections, receive a birthday gift, and enjoy members-only sales events throughout the year.

  • 1 point per dollar
  • Early access to drops
  • Birthday rewards

Custom Orders

Looking for something one-of-a-kind? Work directly with our artisans to create custom ceramics, personalized candles, or bespoke textile pieces. Perfect for weddings, corporate gifts, or that special piece for your home.

  • Direct artisan collaboration
  • 4-6 week lead time
  • Wedding & event specialists

Wholesale

Stock your boutique, hotel, or spa with Ember & Oak products. We offer competitive wholesale pricing, flexible minimum orders, and dedicated account management for retail partners across the country.

  • Competitive wholesale pricing
  • Low minimums
  • Dedicated account manager

Gift Cards

Not sure what to pick? Our digital gift cards let your loved ones choose their own favorites. Available in amounts from $25 to $250, delivered instantly via email with a personalized message.

  • $25 - $250 amounts
  • Instant email delivery
  • Never expires

Have a Special Request?

We love working with customers on custom projects. Get in touch and let us bring your vision to life.
