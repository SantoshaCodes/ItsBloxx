Our Story

Creativity Meets Strategy Since 2019

We started Spark with one belief: great brands are not born -- they are built through intentional strategy, bold design, and relentless execution.

Our Journey Founded June 2019

From Side Project to Full-Service Agency

Spark Creative Agency began as a partnership between two friends who were frustrated by agencies that prioritized flash over function. Maya Chen, a brand strategist with a decade of Fortune 500 experience, and Leo Torres, a self-taught designer and full-stack developer, joined forces in a coworking space in downtown Austin. Their first client was a local coffee roaster who needed a complete rebrand. Within six months, that client's revenue had doubled. Word spread fast. Today, Spark is a team of 15 creatives, strategists, and marketers who have delivered over 200 projects for clients ranging from scrappy startups to established enterprises. Our approach has not changed since day one: listen deeply, think strategically, design boldly, and measure everything. We believe every business has a story worth telling -- and we are here to help you tell it in a way that moves people to action. Whether you need a brand refresh, a high-converting website, or a full-scale digital marketing engine, we bring the same passion and precision to every engagement.
